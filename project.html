3-Mechanical engineering



Mechanical Engineering is a specialized branch of engineering that focuses on the design, analysis, and manufacturing of mechanical systems. It involves a wide range of applications, from creating machines and tools to developing energy systems and heating and cooling technologies. Engineers in this field work with materials, mechanics, thermodynamics, and robotics to create products that improve daily life and industrial processes. Mechanical Engineering is essential for the development of innovative technologies used in sectors like automotive, aerospace, energy, and manufacturing. It ensures the efficient performance and durability of machines and systems
